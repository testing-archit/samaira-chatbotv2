## Smart Tax Harvesting
Octaraa automates tax-loss harvesting for your family's portfolio. It automatically scans your mutual fund and stock holdings to identify opportunities to sell at a loss to offset capital gains tax, and reinvests the funds to maintain your asset allocation. This can save you up to ₹1,00,000 in short-term capital gains tax annually without any manual effort.

## Family Wealth Dashboard
Octaraa offers a unified Family Wealth Dashboard where the primary breadwinner can view the aggregated net worth, asset allocation, and goal progress of the entire family (spouse, kids, parents) on a single screen. This eliminates the need to log into multiple different broker accounts for different family members.
